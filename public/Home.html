Our Foundation

Mission

To produce excellent entrepreneurs with social commitment through a set of well-formulated training along with the culture of research and development among the students and faculty of this institution.

Vision

To become a centre of excellence in the field of Innovation and Entrepreneurship (I&E), promoting creativity, out-of-the-box thinking, startup culture and bringing out socially viable startups that contribute to national development.

About Us

Gravity is the Centre for Innovation, Entrepreneurship and Technology, committed to empower Entrepreneurs and fostering a Startup ecosystem within the institution. Read more...

Gravity is the Centre for Innovation, Entrepreneurship and Technology, committed to empowering Entrepreneurs and fostering a Startup ecosystem within the institution. We serve as a strategic support system, guiding students through every stage of their Entrepreneurial journey from Ideation and Validation to Development and Launch. By offering structured Mentorship, access to industry networks, startup workshops and Incubation support, we help transform ideas into impactful venture. Read less

Our ecosystem includes:

Idea & Validation

We help students identify real-world problems and refine their ideas through brainstorming sessions, design thinking workshops and expert feedback.

Mentorship & Guidance

With access to a network of experienced entrepreneurs, industry leaders and faculty members, students receive one-on-one guidance tailored to their startup journey.

Product Development

Gravity-CIET supports the transition from concept to prototype by facilitating access to technical resources, labs and collaborative development teams.

Incubation Support

Promising ideas get access to incubation facilities, including co-working spaces, technical infrastructure and administrative support.

Startup Compliance

Guidance on company registration, intellectual property rights and regulatory compliance ensures that student navigates the legal aspects of entrepreneurship confidently.

Networking Opportunities

We ensure that students are able to connect with startup ecosystems, alumni entrepreneurs, accelerations and industry bodies to expand their Reach and Collaboration potential.

Skill Development

Regular sessions on entrepreneurship, marketing, finance, branding and leadership equip students with essential skills for building and scaling startups.

Patenting

We simplify the patent process by supporting students in filing and securing Intellectual Property Rights, ensuring their Innovations are protected under National and International Standards.

Our Initiatives

Our initiatives are thoughtfully designed to cultivate innovation, research, creativity, and social impact across all levels of learning. Read more...

Our initiatives foster innovation, research, creativity and social impact at all learning levels. From Research Hub and Design Hub to Women Empowerment through Entrepreneurship, each platform nurtures growth. Outreach programs spark curiosity in school students, while Tea & Talk promotes collaboration. The Incubation Centre and IPR Cell support startups and protect ideas. Together, these hubs build an inclusive ecosystem that inspires future-ready innovators, leaders and changemakers. Read less

Our key Initiatives:

IPR Cell

IPR Cell promotes innovation and legal awareness by guiding students and faculty on patents, copyrights and trademarks. It offers full support in protecting ideas, fostering creativity and ensuring compliance with IPR regulations.

TBIC

Technology-Based Incubation Centre supports early-stage startups with infrastructure, mentorship and resources. It transforms innovative ideas into market-ready solutions, fostering entrepreneurship, accelerating growth and driving impactful technological advancements in a supportive, innovation-driven ecosystem.

Idea Cafe

A relaxed post-class meet-up where students share ideas over tea. This cozy space encourages open conversations, sparks creativity and builds a community where every voice matters and every idea can grow into something impactful.

Innovation school

A unique blend of innovation and community outreach, this hub conducts interactive tech sessions for school studentsâ€”sparking curiosity, building skills and nurturing creativity to shape young minds into confident, future-ready problem solvers.

WEED

Women Empowerment Through Entrepreneurship Development equips women with practical skills in business, finance and marketing. It fosters innovation, mentorship and a supportive ecosystem to help women launch sustainable ventures and drive inclusive community growth.

The Research-Hub

Research Hub fosters innovation, inquiry and academic excellence through training in research methodologies, data analysis and collaboration. It empowers individuals to conduct impactful research, solve real-world problems and contribute to sustainable knowledge-driven growth.

The Design-Hub

Design Hub fosters creativity, design thinking and practical skills through sessions in UI/UX, graphic design and prototyping. It empowers students to solve real-world problems with aesthetic, functional solutions through mentorship and collaboration.
