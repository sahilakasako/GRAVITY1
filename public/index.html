Our Foundation

Mission

To produce excellent entrepreneurs with social commitment through a set of well-formulated training along with the culture of research and development among the students and faculty of this institution.

Vision

To become a centre of excellence in the field of Innovation and Entrepreneurship (I&E), promoting creativity, out-of-the-box thinking, startup culture, and bringing out socially viable startups that contribute to national development.

About Us

Gravity is the Centre for Innovation, Entrepreneurship and Technology, committed to empowering aspiring Entrepreneurs and fostering a Startup ecosystem within the institution. We serve as a strategic support system, guiding students through every stage of their Entrepreneurial journey from Ideation and Validation to Development and Launch. By offering structured Mentorship, access to industry networks, startup workshops and Incubation support. Our comprehensive ecosystem includes:

Ideation & Problem Validation:

We help students identify real-world problems and refine their ides through brainstorming sessions, design thinking workshops and expert feedback.

Mentorship & Guidance:

With access to a network of experienced entrepreneurs, industry leaders and faculty members, students receive one-on one guidance tailored to their startup journey.

Prototype & Product Development:

Gravity CIET supports the transition from concept to prototype by facilitating access to technical resources, labs and collaborative development teams.

Incubation Support:

Promising ideas get access to incubation facilities, including co-working spaces, technical infrastructure and administrative support.

Legal & Startup Compliance:

Guidance on company registration, intellectual property rights and regulatory compliance ensures students navigate the legal aspects of entrepreneurship confidently.

Networking Opportunities:

We connect students with startup ecosystems, alumni Entrepreneurs, Accelerations and industry bodies to expand their Reach and Collaboration potential.

Skill Development:

Regular sessions on entrepreneurship, marketing, finance, branding and leadership equip students with essential skills for building and scaling a startups.

Patenting:

We simplify the patent process by supporting students in filing and securing Intellectual Property Rights, ensuring their Innovations are protected under National and International Standards.
